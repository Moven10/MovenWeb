Move To Earn Events

Move To Earn Events

A Move-To-Earn event challenges you to stay active, hit your fitness milestones, and earn rewards along the way. These virtual challenges let you compete on a global scale for large prizes, and the best part? Winners are chosen at random—simply by participating—giving everyone an equal shot at victory. It's fitness, fun, and a whole lot of motivation rolled into one!
