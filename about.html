We Wanted to Give Back to Our Community

That's why we created Move to Earn—a way to turn every step, ride, and swim into something bigger. Stay active, earn rewards, and be part of a community that moves with purpose. Because at Moven, every effort counts.

Moven is everywhere you move

Moven events can be joined from anywhere in the world, keeping athletes connected no matter where they train. But for now, major events like Moven Marathons, cycling races, swimming competitions, and golf tournaments take place on Australian soil. We’re working on taking them global—stay ready.

Move To Earn Events

Move To Earn Events are available from anywhere across the globe, allowing you to participate no matter where you are. Whether you're in the city, at the beach, or in the mountains, as long as you're moving during the event period, you’re part of the Moven community.
